## About This Achievement

BEST IT Festival is an annual technology event organized by BEST Gliwice at Silesian University of Technology. The game jam challenged participants to create innovative games within a limited timeframe.

### The Game - Electro Exile

Electro Exile is a unique puzzle-platformer where you control a battery that must manage its stored electricity while navigating through a world filled with objects that drain its power.

### Game Features

- **Resource Management**: Balance your electricity usage carefully
- **Environmental Hazards**: Various objects drain power at different rates
- **Strategic Gameplay**: Plan your route to conserve energy

### Team Achievement

Created with a team of 6 people, showcasing our ability to collaborate effectively under time pressure and deliver a polished game concept.
